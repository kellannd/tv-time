What is Once Upon a Time?

Once Upon a Time is a fantasy drama television series that aired from 2011 to 2018 on ABC network for 7 seasons with a total of 156 episodes. The show follows the story of Emma Swan, a young woman who is upended from her ordinary life in the modern world to the fictional realm of Storybrooke because of her son. She is introduced to fairy tales and magic, and where she must break a curse to save her family.

She faces off against her biological son's adoptive mother, Regina Mills aka the Evil Queen, who transported the entire town of Storybrooke from the Enchanted Forest to the real world and erased their memories of their true identities, which were their fairytale counterparts. Emma, with the help of her son Henry, must recover everyone's, including her parents, (Snow White and Prince Charming) memories and save Storybrooke from the Evil Queen's control. The show explores themes of family, love, and the battle between good and evil, with a mix of classic fairy tale characters and original storylines.
